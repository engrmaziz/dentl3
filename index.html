How We Work

Your journey to
a perfect smile.

01
Consultation & Digital Scan
We begin with a comprehensive consultation and 3D digital scan. No X-ray discomfort, just accurate data to inform a personalised plan.
02
Smile Design Preview
Using Digital Smile Design software, we show you exactly what your new smile will look like before any treatment starts.
03
Personalised Treatment Plan
We build a step-by-step roadmap tailored to your goals, timeline, and budget. Zero surprises, complete transparency.
04
Precision Treatment
Our specialists execute with clinical precision, using minimally invasive techniques for maximum comfort and lasting results.
05
Aftercare & Maintenance
Your care doesn't end when treatment does. We provide a tailored aftercare programme and regular check-ins to protect your investment.
